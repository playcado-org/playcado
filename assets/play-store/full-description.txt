Playcado is a beautiful way to watch your personal movie and TV collection on your phone or tablet. It works with Jellyfin, your own private media server.

HIGHLIGHTS

• Browse your library -- Pick up where you left off with Continue Watching, see what's coming up next, and discover recently added movies and shows. Search makes finding anything fast.

• Smooth video playback -- Watch your movies and TV shows with great quality. Switch between audio tracks and subtitles on the fly. Skip past show intros with one tap. A handy mini player lets you browse for something else without stopping your show.

• Watch offline -- Download movies and episodes to your device so you can watch without an internet connection. Downloads keep going even if you switch apps.

• Cast to your TV -- Send videos from your phone to your Chromecast or Android TV. Playcado finds Cast devices on your network automatically.

• Multiple servers -- Connect to more than one Jellyfin server and switch between accounts easily. Your login is stored safely on your device.

• Make it yours -- Pick from 13 accent colors to give the app your own look. Dark theme makes for cozy late-night watching.

• Your privacy matters -- No ads, no tracking, no data collection. Playcado is open source and always will be.

NOTE
You need a Jellyfin media server (version 10.8 or newer) already set up to use Playcado. It does not provide its own content.
